MIT OPEN (TECHTATVA)
Manipal's version of the 'Olympics of Programming Competitions'. MIT Open was an ICPC-style online competitive coding challenge that aimed to expose students to an algorithmic programming -contest experience and hone necessary skill-set requirements for the corporate world.
CODENTINE
For all the enthusiastic coders out there! Get ready to race against time; up your game in the arena of competitive programming and prepare yourself for campus placements by taking part in our biannual competitive programming contest!
INTERNSHIP TALK
An interactive talk session by seniors and alumni with corporate internships and placements to enlighten our community members about the testing process, interviews, resume-building, preparation for the same as well as share their personal experience and journey.
